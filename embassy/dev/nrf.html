Embassy nRF HAL

The Embassy nRF HAL is based on the PACs (Peripheral Access Crate) from nrf-rs.

Timer driver

The nRF timer driver operates at 32768 Hz by default.

Peripherals

The following peripherals have a HAL implementation at present:

  • PWM

  • SPIM

  • QSPI

  • NVMC

  • GPIOTE

  • RNG

  • TIMER

  • WDT

  • TEMP

  • PPI

  • UARTE

  • TWIM

  • SAADC
